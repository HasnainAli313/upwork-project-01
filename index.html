About Captain Appliance

Captain Appliance is a local, family-owned and operated, Christian business dedicated to meeting all your appliance repair needs. We are committed to serve our neighbors and home town community with guaranteed, high quality parts, personal scheduling, and friendly, reliable service.


Our family launched Captain Appliance to revive the meaning of service. After many personal experiences of receiving mediocre service, ignored appointments and confusing pricing, we agreed that our community deserves dedicated service where the standard is raised and customers get more than they expect. We are eager to meet or exceed your expectations and gain your confidence because to us, service means providing care, assistance, help, excellence, and quality to you, AND earning respect, confidence and trust from you, our clients. That's why our slogan is "At YOUR Service."


We are proud to employ highly skilled, pre-screened and insured service technicians who care about your home, your family and all your appliance needs.
